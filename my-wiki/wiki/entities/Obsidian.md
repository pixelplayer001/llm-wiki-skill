# Obsidian

**Obsidian** is a Markdown-based knowledge vault app. In the llm-wiki stack it is the usual editor for `wiki/` and `raw/`, and hosts the **`LLM Wiki Audit`** community plugin under `plugins/obsidian-audit/` (see [[LLM Wiki Knowledge Base Pattern]] and [[summaries/readme]]).
